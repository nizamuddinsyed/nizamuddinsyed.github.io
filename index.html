Drone - Gesture Controlll & Face Following

Demo

Control drone using gesture recognition algorithms and enable facial tracking for autonomous following.

In this project, a drone is controlled using gesture recognition algorithms and facial tracking capabilities. Utilizing OpenCV and Python, the system is designed to interpret specific hand or body gestures as commands for the drone's movement, such as take-off, landing, or changing direction. Additionally, the drone is equipped with facial tracking features, allowing it to autonomously follow a targeted individual by recognizing and locking onto their face. The combination of gesture-based control and facial tracking provides a seamless and interactive experience for controlling the drone.

  • Technologies - OpenCV, Python
  • Role - Data Scientist
